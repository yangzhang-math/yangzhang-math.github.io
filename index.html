About myself

Welcome to my homepage!

Currently I am a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of California, Irvine, working with Prof. Katya Krupchyk.

I was a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Washington, Seattle, working with Prof. Gunther Uhlmann, from 2020-2024.

Before this, I obtained my Ph.D from Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Plamen Stefanov.

Research interest

My primary research interests are inverse problems arising in imaging sciences as well as in various areas of physics and engineering, including

  • Inverse problems for nonlinear hyperbolic equations.
  • Inverse problems arising in nonlinear acoustic imaging.
  • Inverse problems for integral transforms arising in medical imaging.
  • Rayleigh waves and Stonely waves in linear elasticity.
